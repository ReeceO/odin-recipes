Vinaigrette Dressing


Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup vinegar of your choice
  • 2 to 3 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • 1 and 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • pinch of black pepper
  • 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Process:

  1. Combine vinegar, mustard, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a medium bowl.
  2. Slowly pour in the oil while vigorously whisking until all the ingredients are fully combined.

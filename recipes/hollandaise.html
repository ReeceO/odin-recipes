Hollandaise Sauce


step by step instrucitons for a classic hollandaise sauce.


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butte
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • Dash cayenne pepper
  • Finely chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Process:

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan until it is hot and foaming, and keep warm. Do not brown.
  2. Place a medium saucepan with about an inch of hot water over low heat and bring it to a simmer.
  3. In a stainless steel bowl vigorously whisk the egg yolks with the lemon juice until the mixture is thickened.
  4. Place the bowl over the simmering water and continue whisking.
  5. Drizzle the melted butter into the egg yolk mixture in a slow, steady stream while whisking. Continue whisking until the sauce has thickened. Dont let the bowl sit in the water or let the sauce get too hot. If the sauce starts to curdle, try whisking in a small amount of boiling water a half-teaspoon at a time.
  6. Remove the sauce from the heat and whisk in the salt and cayenne pepper.
